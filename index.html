About myself

Who I am

Hi! I am Durga Venkatesh, an experienced UX Specialist and a graduate of Metallurgical & Materials Engineering at IIT Roorkee.

With years of experience scaling consumer and enterprise products, I have transitioned from individual contribution to strategic design leadership. My approach is rooted in systems thinking, data-informed decision making, and an unwavering commitment to quality. I believe that the best products are built when empathy for the user intersects seamlessly with business objectives.

Outside of crafting scalable design systems and mentoring fellow designers, I'm an avid dreamer who loves action games. I frequently explore new tools and methodologies to push the boundaries of digital experiences.
